🐾 Dog OK · Best Stretch
Option 1
Riverfront Park
Fort Smith · Flat paved trail along Arkansas River
Flat, paved trail right along the Arkansas River. Best dog stretch of the drive — open space, shade, and river views. Let the pup run before 2 more hours in the car.
Park at Riverfront Dr. Bring water — July heat starts early.
🐾 Dog OKQuick Look
Option 2
Fort Smith National Historic Site
Historic frontier courthouse · Free · Outdoor grounds
Frontier courthouse and famous gallows from the "Hanging Judge" era. Walk the outdoor grounds with the pup for free. A quick, fascinating history stop right in the city.
Grounds are always open. Interior museum if you want more.
Quick Stop
Option 3
Coffee + Fuel Up
Fort Smith downtown · Grab and go
Hit a local coffee shop, top off the tank, use a real restroom, and stretch legs for 15 minutes before the next 2-hour leg to Texarkana. Fort Smith has several good cafés downtown.
Onyx Coffee Lab has a Fort Smith location — same roaster as NWA.
📍 Texarkana Stop — 12:00 PM (~1 hr) 3 options
Must Do🐾 Dog OK
Option 1
Texas State Line
State Line Ave · Free · Always open
The state line runs down the middle of the street and through the Federal Building — the only U.S. courthouse shared between two states. Straddle TX and AR with the pup for the photo.
15–20 min stop. Park right on State Line Ave.
4.8★🐾 Dog OK
Option 2
Bringle Lake Park
15 min north · Open 7am–10pm
Gorgeous lakefront park with trails and hidden fairy doors in the trees. Best dog walk stretch of the whole trip. Let the pup run before 2.5 more hours in the car.
30-min walk. Bring water — July heat!
Quick Look
Option 3
Perot Theatre
221 Main St · Historic exterior
Beautiful 100-year-old restored theatre in downtown Texarkana. Worth a walk-by on the way to/from the state line. Tours available weekdays 10am–5pm.
10-min exterior stop. Free to admire.
